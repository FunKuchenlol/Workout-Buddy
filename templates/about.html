About Workout-Buddy

What started as a simple school project quickly turned into something much more meaningful. Workout-Buddy grew from lines of code into a passion project – something I'm truly proud of 💻❤️.

I didn’t just want to build “an app.” I wanted to create something helpful, something that supports people in reaching their fitness goals and makes it a little more fun along the way.

Workout-Buddy is my little digital baby – always learning, growing, and improving. And who knows? Maybe it’ll turn into something even bigger 💪✨.
